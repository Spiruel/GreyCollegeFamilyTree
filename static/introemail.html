The Grey College Family Tree is now online! It's been a long time in the making, but is now ready for public release.


In order to build up information about college families, your participation is required. Please check out the website at www.greycollegefamilytree.co.uk
If various sections of your college family sign up - the system is completely automatic and connects everything together right for you, on a webpage.
We will be the first college to create such a recorded family, so let's make the most of it!


You will need to register yourself as both a parent and as a child (unless your family register you first). Family members will then receive emails in which they can verify their place in the family tree.


This project can only get off the ground with the participation of college members, through family registrations. With so many people in the college, we have the opportunity to build something really special here.
 
 
 
 
 
More Information
 

There are boxes of chocolates up for grabs for the largest college family at the end of a few weeks (ranked by most number of generations and number of branches). So make sure you get as many family members signed up as possible!


The family tree has been a big project, and as such will not be devoid of bugs! If anything doesn't make sense, or goes horribly wrong, please let me know as soon as possible using the contact link at the bottom of the email.
If you have an exciting ideas for new features on the webpage, please do not hesitate to let me know.

Thanks for reading and I hope you enjoy!
